About

Who I Am

I'm a third-year medical student at USC Keck School of Medicine with a deep interest in pulmonary & critical care and transplant hepatology. I'm drawn to the diagnostic complexity of medicine — the moments where clinical judgment, data, and probability intersect.

Outside of medicine, I like building and optimizing things. Whether it's a research protocol, a dataset, or a website — I'm most alive when I'm working on something with real stakes.

Before medical school, I spent two years doing oncology research at USC Alfred E. Mann School of Pharmacy, where I developed a foundation in laboratory science and literature synthesis.
